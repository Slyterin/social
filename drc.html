About DRC

DRC, also known as Democratic Republic of Congo, is located in Africa. It lies in the Torrid Zone. The Equator passes through the Northern DRC. It was ruled by Belgium until 1960. At first it was called Belgian Congo. In 1971 it changed into Zaire. Again in 1971 it changed into Democratic Republic of Congo. Its present capital is Kinshasa.
